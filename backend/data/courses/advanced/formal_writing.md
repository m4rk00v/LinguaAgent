# Formal Writing

## Register Differences
- Informal: "I wanna talk about..." → Formal: "I would like to discuss..."
- Informal: "Thanks a lot" → Formal: "Thank you for your consideration"
- Informal: "ASAP" → Formal: "At your earliest convenience"

## Email Structure
1. Greeting: "Dear Mr./Ms. [Name],"
2. Purpose: "I am writing to inquire about..."
3. Body: Clear, concise paragraphs
4. Closing: "I look forward to your response."
5. Sign-off: "Kind regards," / "Sincerely,"

## Common Mistakes
- Wrong: "Hey, I need the report" → Correct: "Dear [Name], I would appreciate receiving the report."
- Wrong: "FYI, the meeting is moved" → Correct: "Please be advised that the meeting has been rescheduled."
- Avoid contractions in formal writing: "don't" → "do not"
