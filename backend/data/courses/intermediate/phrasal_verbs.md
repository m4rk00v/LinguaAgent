# Common Phrasal Verbs

## Daily Life
- Wake up: "I wake up at 7 AM."
- Get up: "I get up immediately after my alarm."
- Turn on / Turn off: "Turn off the lights before bed."
- Pick up: "Can you pick up the phone?"
- Put on / Take off: "Put on your jacket, it's cold."

## Work & Study
- Look up: "Look up the word in the dictionary."
- Figure out: "I need to figure out this problem."
- Give up: "Don't give up, keep trying!"
- Hand in: "Hand in your homework by Friday."
- Come up with: "She came up with a great idea."

## Social
- Get along with: "I get along with my neighbors."
- Look forward to: "I look forward to seeing you."
- Run into: "I ran into an old friend yesterday."

## Common Mistakes
- Wrong: "I look forward to see you" → Correct: "I look forward to seeing you"
- Wrong: "I must figure it up" → Correct: "I must figure it out"
