# Basic Verbs — Present Simple

## Structure
- Affirmative: Subject + verb (+ s/es for he/she/it)
- Negative: Subject + don't/doesn't + verb
- Question: Do/Does + subject + verb?

## Examples
- I work every day.
- She doesn't like coffee.
- Do you speak English?

## Common Mistakes
- Wrong: "She don't like" → Correct: "She doesn't like"
- Wrong: "He work here" → Correct: "He works here"
- Wrong: "Do she speak?" → Correct: "Does she speak?"
