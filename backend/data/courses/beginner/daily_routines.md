# Daily Routines

## Vocabulary
- Wake up, get up, take a shower, get dressed
- Have breakfast / lunch / dinner
- Go to work / school
- Come home, watch TV, go to bed

## Time Expressions
- In the morning / afternoon / evening
- At night
- Every day / week / month

## Common Mistakes
- Wrong: "I am wake up at 7" → Correct: "I wake up at 7"
- Wrong: "I go to the bed" → Correct: "I go to bed"
- Wrong: "In the night" → Correct: "At night"
